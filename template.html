About Sean

Sean McMahon is a rising senior at Bucknell University purusing a Bachelor of Science Degree in Physics. In the summer of 2014 he did research in the field of theoretical statistical mechanics and has an interest in computational physics. He plans to apply to graduate school in the coming year and pursue a Ph.D in Physics. Sean's personal interests include computer science, music, and baseball, particuarly sabermetrics.
